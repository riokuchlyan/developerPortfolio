What is the importance of producers in an ecosystem?

All energy enters food webs and ecosystems via producers.

What is the the 10% rule in ecology?

As you move up each trophic level, the amount of energy is 10% of the trophic level below it.

What portion of the electromagnetic spectrum is used for photosynthesis?

Visible light

What can light do when it strikes a surface?

It can reflect, be absorbed, or be transmitted.

What is the role of pigments in photosynthesis?

The importance of pigment in photosynthesis is that it helps absorb the energy from light.

What are the accessory pigments and what is their role?

Accessory pigments help plants absorb additional light.
