Embed Video Into Website Tutorial

This tutorial will show how to embed videos from popular services such as YouTube and Vimeo into your HTML file!

Step 1

The first step is to go to your preferred video service.

Step 2

Then navigate to the video you want to embed.

Step 3

The option to embed should be somewhere in the video. In YouTube, it is in the share section.

Step 4

Copy the embeding code. Usually, it starts with an iframe tag.

Step 5

Paste the copied text into your HTML file.

Step 6

After completing the above steps, you have successfuly embeded a video into a website!
